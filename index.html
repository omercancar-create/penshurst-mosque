Our Story &
Heritage

Penshurst Mosque is home to the Australian Islamic Society of Bosnia and Herzegovina — one of three Bosnian mosques in Sydney. Rooted in the experiences of Bosnian immigrants who arrived in Australia in the late 1970s, our community has built a place of worship that is as much a cultural anchor as it is a spiritual one.

We serve worshippers in three languages — Arabic, English, and Bosnian — and welcome all Australians through our doors. Over 35 years of peaceful coexistence in the St George region reflects the values we hold dear: faith, family, and belonging.

"We are open to dialogue. We have been good neighbours for over 30 years."

— Imam Ensar Cutahija, Penshurst Mosque

Our Mosque &
The Rebuild

The current building has welcomed worshippers for over three decades. Now, with council approval secured and a construction certificate issued in early 2025, we are preparing to transform the mosque with a new minaret and domed roof — honouring our heritage while building for the next generation.

Community
Programs

Beyond worship, Penshurst Mosque is a hub for Bosnian heritage, interfaith dialogue, and genuine community connection in the St George area.

Annual Event

Annual Open Mosque Day

Every year we open our doors to all Australians for a day of interfaith dialogue, cultural exchange, and warm Bosnian hospitality. Come meet the congregation, ask questions, and experience our community firsthand.

Held annually — all welcome
Monthly

Sunday Community Barbecue

On the last Sunday of each month, the Bosnian Women's Association hosts a community barbecue — Bosnian cevapi, roast lamb, and homemade desserts, with proceeds supporting those in need. Neighbours and strangers alike are welcome.

Last Sunday each month · All welcome
Youth

Bosnian Youth Network

Our young community takes action. In 2019, the Bosnian Youth Network raised over $4,000 and distributed 200 sanitary packs to Sydney's homeless at Martin Place — their inaugural project, triple the original fundraising target.

Based at Penshurst & Smithfield
Every Sunday

Bosnian Ethnic School

Located in the Musafirhana building at 447 Forest Road, Bosnian language classes run every Sunday from 1PM to 3PM during school term — keeping culture and language alive for the next generation, supported by the AISBH.

Sundays 1–3 PM · During school term
